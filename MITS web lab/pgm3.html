About Mulanthuruthy

Mulanthuruthy is a charming village nestled in the Ernakulam district of Kerala, India. Known for its lush greenery and serene landscapes, Mulanthuruthy embodies the rich cultural heritage of Kerala, blending traditional values with the vibrancy of modern life.

The village is surrounded by beautiful landscapes, featuring rolling hills,lush paddy fields, and winding waterways that reflect the tranquility of rural Kerala. The nearby backwaters are a haven for nature lovers, offering opportunities for boating, bird watching, and experiencing the unique ecosystem that thrives in these waters.

Attractions

  • Mulanthuruthy Church: A historical church with stunning architecture.
  • Local Temples: Experience the spiritual vibe of traditional Kerala temples.
  • Nature attractions:The paddy fields in Mulanthuruthy are a stunning patchwork of vibrant green, shimmering under the sun, and embody the essence of Kerala's agrarian beauty, where traditional farming practices sustain the local community.And enjoy the serene beauty of the backwaters nearby.
